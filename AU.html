WHO ARE WE?

As locals, we know the best places to see and experience in the Philippines, which we share through a

definitive collection of Metro Manila travel recommendations. Through in-depth articles, we assist

you in learning more about the Metro, including where to go, what to do, how to get around, where to stay, and how to

organize an itinerary, to ensure that your trip to Manila would be as memorable and fulfilling as possible.



MISSION

Providing affordable travel guides that should enable us to share our knowledge about Metro Manila for the

less fortunate travelers out there,such as newbies, first-timers, lost and confused, shy and introverted

people who have trouble interacting with others, and those who have trouble navigating.



VISION

Encourage both local and foreign travelers to visit Metro Manila more, and the Metro will be the preferred

destination for sustainable, uniquely diverse and fun travel.
