Black Market



Black Market often has famous DJs playing various types of music each night.

If you're in the mood for some reggae, funk, downtempo, and Afro music, look out for their Bad Decisions night on Wednesdays.



Friday’s The Drop night will have you dancing to the beats of trap music, garage, hip hop, and drum & bass.

Saturday’s Black Out is reserved for techno, electro, Dub, and UK funky sounds.The club features another lounge-bar and across the street is a food truck for some late-night grub.



Geared towards the millennials, urban, and young professionals, Black Market Manila doesn't have charge charges for regular club events. Alcohol price is typical of clubs.

Club ZZYZX in Manila



Good setting, extraordinary lighting, and a free entry are what makes Club ZZYZX definitely worth a visit.

The club is open till 6 am in the morning and all through the night they play EDM,



and native music from countries like Japan, China, and Korea.



So if you just need a place to hang out with friends and have a good time, then this place will serve your purpose.

XYLO at the Palace



The Palace in Manila is the most happening nightlife destination in the city.

Together it consists of 2 famous night clubs, both of which give you a spectacular clubbing experience.



One of these clubs named Valkyrie recently went into the renovation and got its name changed to XYLO.

XYLO is a class apart.



The setting of this space is extravagant and lavish. They follow a fixed schedule of performances

and also adhere to a particular dress code without which you will be denied entry in this night club.

The 70s Bistro bar



In an urban day Metropolitan Manila, the 70’s Bistro brings out the evocative sentiments associated with the 1970s.



Set in such a reminiscent setting, this one of a kind bar lights up the vibe of the place with the live music performances

that are presented by the local bands of the area.



For a fun, warm night and delicious dining make sure you visit this place in Quezon City!
